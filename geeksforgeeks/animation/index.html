ELE/Q7106 - Digital Cable Technician


Task:


To Splice (Join) the two end of aptical fiber using Fusion Splice Machine.

Start Exeperiment



STEP 1: a) Click Wear button to wear safety goggles.


b) Click on Cloth to cleanup table surface.


c) Click Add button to take Safety Mat on the table.




STEP 2: a) click Add button to bring cable on the table.


b) Click on cable at given mark to cut the Cable with the desire length.


c) Use round cutter to make circular cut in the cable at 1m length from the end.




STEP 3: a)Use cable sheath stripper to remove the jacket of the cable.


b) Click on the Open Wire to remove one wire from it.


c) Click on the Cloth to clean wires.


d) Click on the Next button to see zoom view of blue wire.




STEP 4: a) Click to Add button to bring protection sleeve on the table.


b) Click on the fusion splice protection sleeve to place protection sleeve over blue wire.



c) Click on Arrow to cut wire.


d) Click on cloth to clean wire.





STEP 5: a) Click on Add button to bring cleaver on the mat.


b) Click on cleaned fiber to put the fiber on to the fiber groove of the cleaver.



c) Click on the fiber holder lid.


d) Click on the Cleaver upcover to close the cleaver.


e) Click on the Cleaver upcover to open the cleaver.


f) Click on the fiber holder lid to open the pin.


g) Click on wire to remove wire.




STEP 6: a) Click on Add button to bring splicer on the mat.


b) Click on splicer to bring zoom view of top splicer.


c) Click on arrow to open splicer.


d) Click on the Pins to open pins.


e) Click on Wires.


f) Click on pins to close the pins.


g) Click on arrow to close the splicer.


h) Click on splicer machine to view zoom view.


i) Click on power key.


j) Click on set key.




STEP 7: a) Click on Add button to bring splicer on the mat.


b) Click on splicer to bring zoom view of top splicer.


c) Click on arrow to open splicer.


d) Click on the pins.


e) Click on the wire.


f) Click on the sleeve.


g) Click on arrow to view heater.


h) Click on arrow to put sleevecoated wire on heater.


i) Click on arrow to press heat button.


j) Click on the heated sleevecoated wire.




STEP 8: a) Click on Add button to Splice Tray.


b) Click on the wires one by one to fit in Splice Tray.
